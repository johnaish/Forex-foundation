Forex Myths Debunked

❌ The Myth ✅ The Reality
Forex is a "Get Rich Quick" scheme. It is a skill-based profession that takes months or years to master.
You need millions to start. You can start with as little as $10 - $50 using micro lots.
You need a degree in Finance. Most successful retail traders are self-taught.
You must trade every day. "No trade" is often the best trade. Patience is key.
