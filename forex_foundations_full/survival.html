Module 4: The Survival Kit (Risk Management) - MOST IMPORTANT

The 3 Golden Rules of Risk Management

  1. 1 The 1% Rule: Never risk more than 1% of total account balance on a single trade.

    Position size = (Account Balance × Risk %) ÷ Stop Loss (pips)

  2. 2 Use Stop Loss (SL): Automatic order that closes trade if market moves against you.

    Protects from account blow-up during news events.

  3. 3 Risk:Reward ≥ 1:2: Risk $10 to make at least $20.

    Even 40% win rate profitable with good RR ratio.

Discipline Note Most beginners fail from lack of discipline, not strategy.

Follow your plan strictly. No revenge trading after losses.

2 Emotion Control

Follow plan, no revenge trading. Journal every trade.

3 Regulated Brokers
