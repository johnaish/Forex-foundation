Module 2 Part: Leverage - The Double-Edged Sword ⚔️

Leverage magnifies BOTH profits AND losses.

What is Leverage?

Leverage allows you to control a large amount of money using a very small deposit (called Margin). While it can magnify profits, it also magnifies losses.

Leverage Formula

Position Value / Margin Required = Leverage

Example: Controlling $10,000 with $100 is 1:100 leverage.

⚔️

Double-Edged Sword

⚠️ Leverage Risks

  • $1:500 leverage = 0.2% move against you wipes account
  • Margin Call when equity < margin requirement
  • Easy to overtrade with "free money" feeling
  • Solution: Start with 1:10 or 1:30
